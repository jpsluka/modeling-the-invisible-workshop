# Challenge Timeline

This repository uses a 40-week season clock.

- Week 1 is the first week of the season.
- Week 1 ends on October 7.
- The season ends after week 40.

For the workshop workflow, the three challenge rounds are organized as staged releases:

| Round | Released data through week | Forecast weeks required |
|---|---:|---:|
| 1 | 8 | 9, 10, 11, 12 |
| 2 | 16 | 17, 18, 19, 20 |
| 3 | 24 | 25, 26, 27, 28 |

Organizers may adjust the release weeks, but every submission must always forecast a specified number of weeks forward, typicall four or five weeks beyond the released data for that round.
